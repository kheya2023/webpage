Work With Us

If you like doing and thinking science, if you're someone who enjoys asking big questions, solving tricky puzzles, and working in an international and interdisciplinary team, you will be happy working with us. Whether you're looking for an internship, a PhD position, or a postdoc role, we offer a supportive, collaborative, and intellectually stimulating environment.

Who We're Looking For

Internships

Ideal for students who want hands‑on research experience. You don’t need to know everything already—just bring curiosity, enthusiasm, willingness to learn, and a sense of adventure. Applications welcome from science students at all levels, from Middle School upwards. We speak French and English.

PhD Students

If you're excited by challenging problems at the interface of physics, biology and chemistry, if you are motivated to do experiments and analyse your own data, if you love solving puzzles, you may want to try a PhD with us. We are always looking for motivated students with Master degree in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering or related fields. Do not hesitate to write with CV and motivation letter. Funding is possible though grants from CENTURI or the Doctoral School.

Postdocs

For researchers ready to push boundaries. We welcome creative thinkers who bring new angles, skills, and ideas. You’ll have room to explore, lead, and grow.

What You Can Expect

  • A friendly and collaborative research culture
  • Freedom to explore ideas that excite you
  • Guidance, mentorship, and room for independence
  • Opportunities to present, publish, and grow professionally
  • A polyglot, interdisciplinary and international team

Interested?

If this sounds like a place for you, we’d love to hear from you. Write to tell us about yourself, your scientific interests and motivation.
